About Me

Hi! I'm Julia Ribeiro, a 19-year-old psychology student and certified bookworm. My love for literature drives me to share my thoughts and insights on my recent reads. Here, you'll find my reviews and opinions on a range of books and genres, particularly focusing on stories that emphasise the complexity of identity, relationship, and character developments. I also love reading about completely unhinged women who make questionable life decisions.

I believe in the power of literature and education to change the world one page at a time, challenging perspectives, igniting discussions, and sharing countless stories from different people all around the world. I hope my reviews will help you discover your next favorite read and provide a space for engaging literary conversation.
